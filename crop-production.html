Crop Production Development

Our Crop Production Development wing focuses on growing high-value crops both on our own farms and on land cultivated by local farmers. We work closely with farmers to produce crops such as sesame, sunflower, cotton, and more, ensuring that these products meet the quality standards required by agro-processors. By bridging the gap between producers and processors, we are helping to build stronger value chains and ensure that Ethiopian crops reach both domestic and international markets.
