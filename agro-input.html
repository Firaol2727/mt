Agro-Input Dealer

To solve the challenges of accessing critical agricultural inputs, our Agro-Input Dealer division sources and supplies the necessary inputs like fertilizers, agrochemicals, and seeds to farmers at the right time and place. We aim to eliminate bottlenecks in the supply chain and ensure that smallholder farmers have the resources they need to maximize productivity and improve their livelihoods.
