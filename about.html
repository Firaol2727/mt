About Us

MA Business Group is a pioneering force in Ethiopia’s agricultural landscape, dedicated to driving transformation and innovation in the sector. Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers and agro-processors by providing them with the essential tools, resources, and support needed to thrive. We focus on delivering sustainable and effective solutions that enhance productivity, boost yields, and ultimately improve livelihoods across the agricultural value chain.

Our Vision

To be Ethiopia's leading force in agricultural development by 2050, revolutionizing access to quality seeds, agro-inputs, and mechanization services. We aim to empower farmers through sustainable practices, driving economic growth and food security across Ethiopia and beyond.

Our Mission

To transform Ethiopian agriculture by providing timely access to high-quality seeds, essential agro-inputs, and mechanization services. We empower smallholder farmers with innovative solutions that enhance productivity, profitability, and sustainability. By collaborating with farmers, agro-processors, and service providers, we strengthen the agricultural value chain, contributing to national food security and economic growth.

Our Divisions

  • Seed Enterprises
    • Seed Enterprises is committed to solving the long-standing issue of access to quality seeds in Ethiopia. By collaborating with research institutions and local agricultural offices, we ensure that farmers receive high-quality seeds tailored to their specific needs. Our efforts are aimed at improving crop yields and resilience, making us a trusted partner for both smallholders and large-scale growers.
  • Mechanization Services Center
    • Our Mechanization Services Center brings modern agricultural machinery to the doorstep of Ethiopian farmers. We offer machinery rental services, operator training, and spare parts supply to reduce labor-intensive practices and enhance productivity. From land preparation to post-harvest processing, we support farmers at every stage of their production journey.
  • Crop Production Development
    • The Crop Production Development division focuses on cultivating high-value crops both on our own farms and through partnerships with local farmers. We specialize in crops such as sesame, sunflower, and cotton, ensuring they meet the quality standards required by agro-processors. By connecting producers and processors, we help build robust value chains that benefit both domestic and international markets.
  • Agro-Input Dealer
    • Our Agro-Input Dealer division addresses the challenges of accessing critical agricultural inputs. We source and supply fertilizers, agrochemicals, and seeds to farmers, ensuring they receive what they need when they need it. Our reliable distribution networks and focused support for smallholder farmers help maximize productivity and improve livelihoods.

Our Commitment

At MA Business Group, we are deeply committed to the growth and success of Ethiopia’s agricultural sector. By addressing the core challenges of seed access, mechanization, crop production, and input supply, we are working tirelessly to transform agriculture in Ethiopia. Our goal is to create a more prosperous and sustainable future for all stakeholders in the agricultural value chain.
